INSTITUTIONAL STRUCTURE

Selebi Phikwe Town Council, in accordance with the Local Government Act 2012, consists of the political wing which is led by the Mayor and the administrative wing which is led by the Town Clerk. Functions of Council are discharged through the following committees:
-Executive Committee
-Finance and General Purposes Committee
-Education, Health and Social Services Committee
-Self Help Housing Agency and Physical Planning Committee
-Performance Monitoring Committee
-Audit Committee
-Local Economic Development Committee

The administration of Council functions is undertaken by the following departments:

-Finance and Development Planning
-Social Welfare and Community Development
-Education
-Physical Planning, Housing and Estate Management
-Architecture and Buildings Services
-Civil and Mechanical Services
-Environmental Health
-Legal Services
-Fire Services
