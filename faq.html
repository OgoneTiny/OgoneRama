  • The Selebi Phikwe Town Council has approximately 10 departments which are: HR and Admin, Finance and Development Planning, Education Socila welfare, Housing and Estate, Architexture and buildings, public health and legal services. And each have their divisions.

  • Selebi Phikwe Town Council was established through the ‘Establishment of the Selebi Phikwe Town Council Order’ under Regulation 4 of the Townships Act of 1965 (CAP.40:02) issued on the 26th of October 1974. The Act provides composition and functions of Council.
